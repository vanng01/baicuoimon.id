About Us

Being a florist is so much more than just creating pretty bouquets. It details, direction and professionalism. It's long weekends and long days. It tears of joy and sore fingers. It's running your hands through the miracle of nature and never getting tired of the beauty. It's a lifestyle that I am so thankful that I have been blessed to live.


Our why is to provide beautifully curated floral arrangements that make your wedding stand alone. We believe every petal has a purpose and high quality blooms paired with hard work create beautiful results. We believe every bride is different, and every event is unique--our why is you!
